# Methodology

Our evaluations are driven by a strict, vendor-skeptical framework designed specifically for small accounting firms using QuickBooks Online. 

## The QASEM Framework
Tools and integrations are not recommended based on popularity or affiliate payouts. They are evaluated using the **QuickBooks Automation Stack Evaluation Model (QASEM)**:

1. **FMR — Failure Mode & Risk:** How and when does this tool break? What is the blast radius?
2. **ATS — Audit Trail Strength:** Can changes be tracked and verified?
3. **IFS — Integration Friction:** How brittle is the connection to QBO and the rest of the stack?
4. **CPCS — Cost Per Client Scaling:** Does the pricing model punish growth?
5. **ADS — Automation Depth:** How deeply does it solve the problem versus just masking it?

Our methodology prioritizes risk containment, auditability, operational maintainability, and commercial sustainability over superficial ease of use. It relies on establishing a definitive **Source of Truth** and enforcing **Idempotency** to prevent cascading failures across the stack.
