## Layer 1: The Decision Surface
**What this page answers:** When is a forced upgrade to QBO Advanced necessary for small firms?
**Decision Summary:** Upgrade to Advanced only when encountering hard class/location limits, requiring custom user roles, or needing Fathom-level reporting integration natively.

## Layer 2: Authority & Trade-Offs
**Cost-Per-Client Scaling (CPCS):** The jump from Plus to Advanced is a massive CPCS hit. Do not upgrade to solve a workflow constraint that could be orchestrated externally via Make for a fraction of the cost.

## Layer 3: Deep Base & Architecture Implications
**Workflow Constraint:** QBO Plus enforces a limit on combined classes and locations. Reaching this limit is a hard operational bottleneck. Firms must map whether to upgrade or migrate (Migration Trigger) based on dimensional reporting needs.
