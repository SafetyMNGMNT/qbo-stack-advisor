# Editorial Standards & Factual Drift Policy

This project operates on strict editorial controls to maintain objectivity, accuracy, and vendor-skepticism.

## Factual Drift Controls
* **No Unsupported Quantitative Claims:** Numbers, limits, and pricing must be cited and verified.
* **Conservative API Claims:** Integration capabilities are described conservatively unless explicitly verified.
* **No Exaggeration:** Product functionality will not be overstated. "Our internal analysis" or similar vague authority language is strictly prohibited unless tied to published data.

## Objectivity Guardrails
* **Independence:** If a non-paying tool is superior for a specific workflow, it will be recommended over a monetized tool.
* **Risk Disclosure:** If a monetized tool carries specific risks or failure modes, those limitations will be stated clearly in the primary evaluation.
* **Continuous Audit:** Published content is subject to periodic review to ensure technical specifications, pricing, and integration capabilities remain accurate.
