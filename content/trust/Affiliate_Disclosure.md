# Affiliate Disclosure & Monetization Policy

This project utilizes a layered monetization model to support operations without compromising editorial integrity.

## Legal & Compliance Framework (UK)
This site operates from the United Kingdom. We comply with the Advertising Standards Authority (ASA) CAP Code and the Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) guidelines regarding the transparent disclosure of affiliate relationships and sponsored content.

## Ethical Monetization Rules
* **No Recommendation Based on Payout:** Affiliate commissions do not dictate rankings, recommendations, or sentiment.
* **Explicit Disclosures:** All pages containing affiliate links carry clear disclosures, compliant with ASA requirements ensuring commercial intent is obvious before engagement.
* **Transparency of Risk:** Monetized tools are subjected to the exact same failure-mode and risk analysis (QASEM) as non-monetized tools. If a paying tool is weaker or riskier in a specific context, that fact is explicitly stated.

## Current Verified Partners
The project currently maintains affiliate or partner relationships with select vendors, which may include: Make, Dext, Rewind, and Method. Recommendations for these platforms remain strictly governed by our Methodology and Editorial Standards.
