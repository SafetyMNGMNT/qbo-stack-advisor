# Traffic & Funnel Architecture Map

This document governs the approved flow of attention from acquisition to monetization.

## 1. Top of Funnel (Acquisition)
*Target: Small firm operators near a stack or migration decision.*
*   **Search Intent (SEO):** High-intent decision pages (e.g., `Zapier_vs_Make.md`, `QBO_vs_Xero.md`, `QuickBooks_Desktop_2023_Sunset.md`).
*   **Targeted Outreach:** Cold email distribution of the Risk Checklist to micro-operators (blogs, small newsletters).
*   **LinkedIn Distribution (Manual):** Extraction of "failure mode" insights from the cornerstone page, posted natively with a link back to the site.

## 2. Middle of Funnel (Engagement & Trust)
*Target: Establishing QASEM methodology and vendor-skepticism.*
*   **The Cornerstone:** `Best_QBO_Tech_Stack.md` acts as the routing hub, establishing the 5-layer model and linking out to specific comparisons.
*   **Decision Deepening:** Users navigate from the cornerstone to specific tool comparisons or migration warnings based on their firm's constraints.

## 3. Lead Capture (Conversion)
*Target: Transitioning anonymous traffic into an owned audience.*
*   **The Choke Point:** Every Cluster A and Cluster B page must include a bottom-of-page CTA routing to `/download-risk-checklist/`.
*   **The Mechanism:** Formspree gating the `QBO_Automation_Risk_Checklist.pdf` in exchange for Name and Email.

## 4. Bottom of Funnel (Monetization)
*Target: Revenue generation via validated methods.*
*   **Layer 1 (Support Revenue):** Ethical, disclosed affiliate links (Make, Dext, Rewind, Method) embedded naturally within the "Decision Deepening" pages.
*   **Layer 2 (Audience Value):** The captured email list (currently held in Formspree).
*   **Layer 3 (Owned Product):** *[Status: Frozen per validation rules]* The Premium Migration Audit Kit. Will be introduced to the email list only after Phase 1 traction is proven.
